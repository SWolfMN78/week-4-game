Crysal Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
You win the game by matching your total score to the randomly generated number displayed. You lose the game if your total exceeds the shown number.
The value of each crystal is hidden from you, but if you lick on it you can get a strong idea of the number.
Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of the crstyals. Good luck, have fun!
